Laxative :-

Foods, compounds, or medication taken to induce bowel movements, most often taken to treat constipation.

Lead :-

An insulated wire with four electrodes that connects the neurosis implanted in the chest in deep brain stimulation to the extension placed under the scalp.

Lessoning :-

A surgical technique used to treat advanced PD. It involves making deliberate, selective damage (a lesion) within particular parts of the brain. There are currently three target sites: the thalamus (thalami), the glob us pallid us (palliation) and the sub thalamic nucleus (Sutherland).

Leisured :-

A dopamine agonist used to treat PD.
